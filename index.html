Bhartiya Antariksh
Hackathon 2025

Welcome to the Bhartiya Antariksh Hackathon 2025 — a nationwide innovation challenge organised by the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) with Hack2skill as the innovation partner.

We are calling upon undergraduates, graduates, postgraduates, and PhD scholars from across India to bring their creativity, passion, and problem-solving skills to address real-world challenges inspired by India’s bold space exploration programs.

This is more than just a hackathon — it’s a mission. A mission where you collaborate with fellow innovators, receive mentorship from leading experts, and build solutions that could contribute to India’s space future.

What is the
Bharatiya Antariksh Hackathon?

The Bharatiya Antariksh Hackathon 2025 is where bold ideas meet real-world space challenges.

As India accelerates its journey in space research and exploration, this hackathon invites the country’s brightest minds to step up and drive the next wave of innovation.

With problem statements directly from ISRO, participants will get the unique opportunity to apply their skills, think creatively, and build solutions that could shape the future of India’s space missions.
